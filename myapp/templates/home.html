  • Monitoring Competition

    Exporters face stiff competition from domestic and global exporters due to lack of information on: trade statistics, product demand, competitive pricing, supply chain trends and evolving buyer behavior.

  • Buyer’s Identification

    Acquiring active importers' contacts is hard in the international trading market. The lack of information on foreign buyers makes exporters wary and jittery about expanding their business globally.

  • Product Flow

    Product flow is dynamic and subject to fluctuations in demand-supply. Uncertainty on product flow due to lack of information on historical transactions can further complicate international trade.

  • Risk Evaluation

    Unfortunately, not all overseas exporters are what they seem. There are many challenges associated with finding the reliable suppliers/sellers like evaluating the authenticity and legitimacy by effectively filtering out fake/scam.

  • Pricing Strategy

    Data helps in pricing strategy by providing valuable insights into market trends, customer behavior, and competitive landscapes, enabling businesses to make informed pricing decisions that align with market dynamics and customer preferences.
